How We Met

It was a sunny afternoon in June when we first crossed paths. The park was bustling with people enjoying the perfect weather, but somehow among the crowd, our eyes met.

You were reading your favorite book under that old oak tree, and I was walking my friend's dog who, as fate would have it, decided to make a beeline straight for you. What started as an awkward apology turned into hours of conversation.

Little did we know that this chance encounter would be the beginning of our story together.
